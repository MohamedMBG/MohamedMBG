## About

Computer science engineering student at EMSI Rabat (Morocco), final year. I build backends and the apps that sit on top of them — mostly Java/Spring Boot on the server, Android and React on the client.

Most of what I ship starts as a vague brief from a real client and ends as software in production. That means I spend as much time on auth, data models, and failure handling as on features. I care about systems that keep working when the network doesn't.

Open to a **software engineering internship** where I can work on real backend or full-stack systems.

- **Working on:** clean layered backends, offline-first mobile, and getting CI/CD out of my way.
- **Currently:** turning [EduLife](https://github.com/MohamedMBG/EduLife) into a real MVP — Spring Boot + PostgreSQL backend, Android + React clients.
- **Ask me about:** structuring a Spring Boot service, syncing SQLite/Room against a remote source, or making Firestore behave.
